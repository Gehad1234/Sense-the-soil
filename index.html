Introduction

There are a lot of variables that make pollute the earth and affect the climate. In our project, we aim to measure the amount of increasing these variables per year, so we measured some harmful gases such as CO2 and CO as independent variables and we also measured the temperature and the soil moisture as dependent variables. Then, we put these sensors into a closed system that simulates reality to collect accurate results, and finally, we connected these sensors to a Wi-Fi module to make wireless communication with a device, and then the collected data will appear on an arranged website.
